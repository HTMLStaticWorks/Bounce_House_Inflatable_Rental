Our Story

Bringing joy and premium entertainment to families since 2018.

How We Started The Fun

What began as a small family-owned business with just two bounce houses has grown into the premier inflatable rental company in the region. We realized early on that event planning is stressful, and we wanted to provide a hassle-free, premium experience.

Today, our massive fleet includes state-of-the-art water slides, intricate obstacle courses, and specialized event games. We take pride in our 100% on-time delivery rate and our rigorous safety protocols.
