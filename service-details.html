Tropical Combo

Bounce House + Slide Combo Unit

Unit Overview

The Tropical Combo is our most requested unit. It features a massive 15x15 jumping area, a basketball hoop inside, and a dual-lane slide that can be used wet or dry.

Designed with safety netting on all sides to allow for excellent ventilation and visibility, parents can easily keep an eye on the fun. The entrance ramp includes a safety bumper, and the roof provides shade during hot summer days.

Key Features

  • Dual Lane Slide
  • Internal Basketball Hoop
  • Wet or Dry Setup
  • Sunshade Roof

Specifications

Dimensions 28'L x 15'W x 15'H
Required Space 32'L x 19'W x 16'H
Power Outlets 1 (110V, 20 Amp)
Max Capacity 8 Kids or 800 lbs
Age Group 3 to 12 Years

Safety Guidelines

Please review these rules before booking.

No Shoes or Sharp Objects
No Food, Gum, or Drinks
Adult Supervision Required
Deflate if Winds Exceed 15mph
